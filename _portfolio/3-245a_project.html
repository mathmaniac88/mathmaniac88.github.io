--- title: "Introduction to Fourier Series and Their Convergence in \(L^p\)" excerpt: "Expository paper done as the final project for Math 245A" collection: portfolio ---

This is an expository paper written for my final project for Math 245A.

We first develop some basic theory of Fourier series, and then we investigate the convergence of Fourier series with respect to the \(L^p\) norms. We give counterexamples in the case of \(p = 1\) and \(p = \infty\), and bound the growth of the Fourier partial sums in those cases. We end with a brief discussion of the Carleson-Hunt theorem
