--- title: "Uniformization and Fuchsian Models of Hyperbolic Riemann Surfaces" excerpt: "Expository paper done as the final project for Math 246C" collection: portfolio ---

This is an expository paper written for my final project for Math 246C.

We use the Uniformization Theorem and universal coverings of Riemann surfaces to classify Riemann surfaces as a quotient. Then, we define Fuchsian groups and prove that every hyperbolic Riemann surface is the quotient of the upper half plane by a Fuchsian group, which gives rise to Fuchsian models of hyperbolic Riemann surfaces.
